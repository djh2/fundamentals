\pts{30}
\begin{problem}

A writing utensil may either be a pen or pencil.  A pen has a color
and an amount of ink, measured in microlitres (\(\mu\)l).  A pencil
has a graphite lead, measured in millimetres (mm).

Five microlitres of ink will write a line seven metres long, while a
graphite lead of 3 millimetres will write a line 22 metres long.

\begin{enumerate}
\item Design a data representation of writing utensils.

\item Give three examples of writing utensils.

\item Design a method for writing utensils that determines how long
  of a line it can write.
\end{enumerate}
\end{problem}
